FAQ

What is Spindig?

Spindig is a vinyl record discovery app. Browse curated crates of handpicked records, swipe to discover new music, preview tracks, and find where to buy.

How do I save a record?

Swipe right on a record to save it. You can find all your saved records in the My Records tab.

How do I preview a track?

Swipe up on a record card to hear a 30-second preview. Tap the preview bar to stop playback.

Where can I buy the records?

Open any saved record and you'll find buy links for Discogs, eBay, and Amazon at the bottom of the page.

Do I need an account?

No. Spindig works without any account or login. Your collection is stored locally on your device.

Can I get back records I passed on?

Close and reopen the app to get a fresh set of crates. Records you previously passed on may reappear in the rotation.

Is Spindig available on Android?

Not yet. Spindig is currently available on iOS only.
