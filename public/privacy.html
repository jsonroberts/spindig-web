Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Spindig is a vinyl record discovery app. We respect your privacy and aim to collect as little data as possible.

What We Collect

Spindig does not require an account. We use an anonymous device identifier (generated randomly on your device) to track your saved records and browsing history within the app. This identifier is not linked to your name, email, or any personal information.

Data Stored on Your Device

  • Saved record IDs (your collection)
  • Seen record IDs per crate (so you don't see the same records twice)
  • Anonymous device ID

Analytics

We use PostHog for anonymous analytics to understand how the app is used (e.g., which features are popular, crash rates). Analytics data is not tied to your identity and does not include personal information.

Third-Party Services

Spindig uses the following third-party services:

  • Discogs API — to fetch album artwork, metadata, and buy links
  • Deezer API — to provide 30-second audio previews
  • PostHog — for anonymous usage analytics

Each service has its own privacy policy. We do not share your personal data with any of these services.

Affiliate Links

When you tap a buy link in Spindig, it may include an affiliate tag (eBay, Amazon). This helps support the app. The affiliate programs may track your purchase but we do not receive any personal information about you from these programs.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, emails, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Photos, contacts, or other device data
  • Advertising identifiers

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Deletion

Since we don't collect personal data, there is nothing to delete. You can clear your app data at any time by deleting and reinstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach out at privacy@digspindig.com.
